Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before working with us. Don't see your question? Ask us directly.

What types of businesses do you work with?

We work with local businesses, restaurants, retail shops, service providers, e-commerce brands, and entrepreneurs across Florida. If you have a business and want to grow it — we can help. We've worked with wood carvers, steakhouses, farmers markets, and everything in between.

How soon will I see results?

Many clients see results within the first 30 days. Our client Tokyo Japanese Steakhouse saw same-day bookings from their first video campaign. Joe Miller at Joe Tiki grew his business 10x within a year. Timelines vary by service, but we move fast and focus on measurable ROI from day one.

What is Freeland Automation and do I need it?

Freeland Automation is our AI-powered service suite that automates your business operations — CRM follow-ups, email sequences, social scheduling, call-center booking, lead nurturing, and more. If you're manually following up with leads, answering the same questions repeatedly, or losing customers because you're too busy to respond fast enough — you need it.

Do you require long-term contracts?

No long-term contracts. We earn your business every month through results. We're confident enough in what we deliver that we don't need to lock you in. You stay because it's working — not because you're trapped.

How much do your services cost?

Pricing is tailored to your business goals and the services you need. We've worked with businesses at all stages — from garage startups to established brands — and we have flexible packages to match. The best way to get accurate pricing is to book a free consultation so we can understand your goals and build a plan that fits your budget.

What makes Freeland Marketing different from other agencies?

Most agencies put you in a queue and send automated reports. We show up. Kevin personally visits clients, films content on-location, and stays hands-on throughout the entire partnership. We also combine marketing services with AI automation — meaning we don't just drive traffic, we help convert and retain it too. Our client results speak louder than any pitch we could make.

Can you handle both my marketing AND automation?

Yes — that's our specialty. We offer full-service packages that combine social media management, paid ads, SEO, and Freeland Automation so everything works together as one unified growth engine. No juggling multiple vendors.

Do you work with businesses outside of Florida?

Yes. While we're based in Palm Coast, FL and have deep community roots here, our digital services and automation buildouts work for businesses anywhere in the United States. Reach out and let's talk.
